VIP Level and Privileges

VIP Level Sheet

VIP Level Spending Range Privileges
Bronze $0 - $99 Basic support, Access to exclusive sales

Gold $200 - $499 Dedicated account manager, Priority support, Access to exclusive sales, Free shipping

Privileges Explanation

  • Basic support: Standard customer support services available to all VIP members.
  • Priority support: Faster response times and priority handling of customer service requests.
  • 24/7 dedicated support: Round-the-clock customer support with dedicated assistance.
  • Access to exclusive sales: Early or exclusive access to sales and promotions not available to non-VIP members.
  • Early product releases: Access to new products before they are available to the general public.
  • Dedicated account manager: A personal representative to handle queries and provide personalized recommendations.
  • Free shipping: Complimentary standard shipping on all orders.
  • Free shipping and returns: Complimentary shipping and return services for all orders.
  • Free global shipping and returns: Complimentary international shipping and return services worldwide.
  • Priority handling of orders: Your orders are processed and shipped with priority.
  • Access to exclusive events: Invitations to exclusive VIP events, product launches, and special experiences.
  • Higher loyalty points earning rate: Earn loyalty points at an accelerated rate on all purchases.
  • Highest loyalty points earning rate: Maximum loyalty points earning rate available in the program.
  • Personal concierge service: A dedicated concierge to assist with all your needs and requests.
  • Access to exclusive collections: Early or exclusive access to limited edition and exclusive product collections.
